About FLOW

  • Start your journey to better habits and inner peace with Flow, your personal habit builder and meditation companion!
  • Flow is a habit builder and meditation app for and targeted towards the new generation for a healthy well-being.
  • Keep track of your daily habits like drinking water, cycling and many more with our frequent reminders.

Our App

Unique Selling Points. (USP)

  • Easy to Use:

    Flow makes forming new habits and meditating effortless with its simple and intuitive interface, perfect for beginners and seasoned users alike!
  • No-Ads:

    We believe that our users should not be distracted with Ads during their self-development sessions.
  • Non-Profit:

    Flow is a non-profit app, focused on your well-being, making mindfulness and habit-building accessible to all, free from hidden agendas!"

Pricing

  • It's FREE, Flow is run by a non-profit organization, our app is free to download for every individual that aims to lead a healthy life.
  • Our app is available to download from popular application stores like Google Playstore and App Store.

Place
